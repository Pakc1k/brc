Executive Summary

$BRC (Barong vs Rangda Coin) is a 30-year digital initiative using mythic imagery from Balinese art to create a dual-token civic ecosystem. Barong represents cleanliness and environmental care; Rangda represents empowerment through education. All experiences are digital for the first 10 years.

1. Myth-Inspired Infrastructure

Barong and Rangda are reimagined as civic identities: Barong leads waste cleanup missions, Rangda leads education and empowerment campaigns. Everything is on-chain, transparent, and built around digital missions and engagement.

3. Technical Architecture

  • Phase 1: Ethereum L2, ERC-1155 relics, digital missions
  • Phase 2: Cosmos SDK, mission VM, proof-of-impact tokens
  • Phase 3: ZK-verification, AI lore bots
  • Phase 4–6: Modular consensus, interchain mesh, civic oracle layers

4. Token Lifecycle

$BRC (Utility Token): Digital mission engine, relic minting, validator rewards.

$RANGDA (Governance Token): Earned via social impact, grants proposal and voting.

  • 20% reserved for contributors (vested over 30 years)
  • 5% of fees fund cleanup and education
  • Impact-based token burns and deflation

5. Digital Impact Systems

  • Mission validation via pledges and digital tasks
  • Grants DAO for Bali-focused impact work
  • Soulbound NFTs for civic progress
  • Open source modules for other chains

6. Long-Term Vision

$BRC becomes a decentralized civic protocol focused on measurable impact, not speculation. Barong and Rangda evolve as symbolic identities to inspire collective cleanups, empowerment, and digital coordination.
